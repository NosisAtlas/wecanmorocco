FOUZIYA MADHOUNI
FOUNDER AND PRESIDENT
@ WE CAN MOROCCO

We can Morocco is an NGO that seeks to provide English and leadership classes through American football. The organization was newly founded in 2021 but managed to launch the first American football education academy in Africa as well as signing strong partnership with the commune of Sidi bouknadel and the star wright foundation.

We Can Morocco was founded and is chaired by Fouziya MADHOUNI, first professional African player of American football and who evolves currently at the WNFC US League with Team Phantom’z.

We can Morocco launched the first edition of access to play clinics on the 15th and the 16th of may 2021 that highlight training camps and coaching clinics for the youth of sidi boukndael and local American football teams .
