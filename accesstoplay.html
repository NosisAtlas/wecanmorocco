Access to play Bootcamp

Our ambition with this bootcamp is to promote the emancipation of young girls in rural areas through American Football. We can Morocco launched the first edition of access to play clinics on the 15th and the 16th of may 2021 that highlight training camps and coaching clinics for the youth and girls of sidi boukndael and local American football teams. This event is organised in partnership with the commune of Sidi bouknadel and the star wright foundation.
