American Football Education Academy

We can Morocco managed to launch the first American football education academy in Africa as well as signing strong partnership with the commune of Sidi bouknadel and the star wright foundation. Through our academy we aim to strengthen the spirit of leadership and initiative among young people from the commune of Sidi Bouknadel, and in particular young girls.

To succeed in our mission, we have established these objectives:

- Promote the emancipation of rural women through strengthening the spirit of leadership.
- Provide access to quality English education.
- Open up the youth of the commune of Bouknadel on a multicultural environment by creating mixed teams.

Our activities scheduled throughout the year are divided into:

- English lesson.
- American Football Lessons.
- Life skills coaching sessions.
- Social monitoring of beneficiaries.
